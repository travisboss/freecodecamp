Dave Mirra speaking at X-Games.

Here is a timeline of Dave's life:

  • 1976 - Born in Chittenango, New York.
  • 1987 - Jones Haro BMX team.
  • 1992 - Turns pro.
  • 1995 - 2009 - Wins a medal at every X-Games except 2006.
  • 2005 - Won best male action sports athlete ESPY.
  • 2008 - Starts competing in rallycross.
  • 2016 - Is found dead in his truck with a self inflicted gun shot wound.
